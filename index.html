Industry Playbooks
🦷 Dental

Selling to Dental Offices

They're drowning in confirmations, no-shows, and new patient follow-up.

Opening:
"How many hours a day does your front desk spend on confirmation calls and rescheduling?"

The pitch:
"We can cut that to near-zero. Our AI handles confirmations, responds to new patient inquiries in under 60 seconds, and fills cancelled slots automatically. You keep your team focused on patient experience."
"What about HIPAA?"
Everything is HIPAA-aware. We don't store PHI outside your systems. All communications log to your practice management software. We can walk through controls with your compliance officer.
"Patient trust is everything."
We're not automating care — we're automating the 47 reminder calls your front desk makes daily. Your team does the care, we handle coordination.
🏠 Real Estate

Selling to Realtors/Teams

Lead response time is everything. They're at showings while leads go cold.

Opening:
"When a new lead comes in from Zillow at 2pm and you're at a showing — how long until they hear from someone?"

The pitch:
"We make that 60 seconds. Our AI responds instantly, qualifies interest, and books the call with you. By the time you're free, they're warm and scheduled."
"My leads need a personal touch."
Absolutely — that's you. We just make sure you get the chance. Right now leads are contacting 3-4 agents. The fastest, most helpful response wins. We make sure that's you.
"I already have a CRM that sends follow-ups."
CRMs send drip emails. Ours reads responses, answers questions, and knows when to escalate. It's a conversation, not a campaign.
🔧 HVAC / Trades

Selling to Trade Companies

Missed calls = lost jobs. They're on ladders while the phone rings.

Opening:
"How many calls go to voicemail when your team is on a job?"

The pitch:
"Every missed call is a lost job. We answer every call, qualify urgency, book the appointment, and dispatch to your team. No more 'sorry we missed you' — just booked revenue."
"Our customers want to talk to a real person."
They want their problem solved. Fast. The AI books them same-day if it's urgent, or schedules for when it makes sense. Then your tech calls to confirm. Personal touch where it counts.

Objections
Objection
"Why can't I just use ChatGPT?"
You can. But having a tool isn't the same as having a system. ChatGPT doesn't connect to your CRM, respond to leads at 2am, or know your escalation rules. We build the system that does.
Objection
"This seems expensive."
Compare it to hiring. A full-time admin is $40K+ with benefits, 40 hours/week, calls in sick. Tend is $24K/year, works 24/7, never misses a lead. The math works.
Objection
"What if it makes mistakes?"
It will, at first. That's why we have human-in-the-loop checkpoints for anything high-stakes. We monitor and tune constantly. Mistakes get caught fast.
Objection
"I've tried AI tools before."
Most AI tools are self-serve — you're on your own. We're the opposite. We build it, run it, fix it. You just see results.
Objection
"Is my data safe?"
Scoped permissions — the AI only touches what it needs. We don't train on your data. Actions are logged and auditable. You own everything.
